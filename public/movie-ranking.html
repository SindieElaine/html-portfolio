My top 3 series of all time...

The last of Us

The Last of Us TV series, based on the acclaimed video game by Naughty Dog, is a gripping post-apocalyptic drama that explores the emotional and physical journey of Joel and Ellie. Set in a world ravaged by a fungal infection that has decimated humanity, the series dives deep into themes of survival, loss, and the enduring bonds formed in the face of devastation. Pedro Pascal delivers a powerful performance as Joel, a hardened smuggler haunted by past trauma, while Bella Ramsey brings depth and vulnerability to Ellie, a teenager who may hold the key to humanity's future. With its cinematic storytelling, faithful adaptation, and emotional impact, The Last of Us stands out as one of the most compelling video game-to-TV adaptations in recent years.

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a critically acclaimed TV series that follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine producer, as he descends into the criminal underworld. Faced with a terminal cancer diagnosis and financial struggles, Walter partners with former student Jesse Pinkman to secure his family's future, but quickly becomes consumed by power, pride, and greed. The show masterfully explores themes of morality, identity, and consequence, with Bryan Cranston delivering an unforgettable performance as the increasingly ruthless anti-hero. With its sharp writing, intense storytelling, and complex characters, Breaking Bad has earned its place as one of the greatest television dramas of all time.

Supernatural

Supernatural is a long-running TV series that follows the thrilling adventures of brothers Sam and Dean Winchester as they hunt demons, ghosts, monsters, and other supernatural creatures. Armed with their father’s journal and a classic ’67 Chevy Impala, the brothers travel across America confronting dark forces and uncovering ancient secrets. The show blends horror, action, and humor with strong themes of family, sacrifice, and destiny. Over its 15-season run, Supernatural built a loyal fanbase and became a cultural phenomenon, thanks in part to the chemistry between Jared Padalecki and Jensen Ackles, who brought depth and heart to the Winchester brothers.
